# Priority Interview

Ask these questions weekly, or when priorities shift. The answers drive PRIORITIES.md.

## Questions

### 1. Product priorities
What are the product priorities right now? Rank P0 → P3.
- Which products are revenue-critical this quarter?
- Are there compliance or security deadlines?
- Any contractual delivery dates?

### 2. Deal dependencies
What deals or partnerships depend on engineering work?
- Partner commitments with dates
- Revenue at risk if work is delayed
- Demo/pilot dates coming up

### 3. Blockers
Who is blocked on what?
- PRs waiting for review or merge
- Decisions that haven't been made
- Access or infrastructure blockers

### 4. What NOT to work on
What should we explicitly avoid this week?
- Parked features
- Deprioritized products
- Items that look urgent by score but aren't truly important

### 5. Changes since last interview
Anything new since last week?
- Meetings that shifted priorities
- Client escalations
- New information that changes the plan

## Output

After the interview, update `.brief/PRIORITIES.md`:
- Tag each item with source: (interview), (scope), (meeting), (kb)
- Rank by: URGENT → NOW → TODAY → IGNORED
- Include reasoning: why this item is at this priority level

Save raw interview notes to `.brief/raw/interview-YYYY-MM-DD.md`.
